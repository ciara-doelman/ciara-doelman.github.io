Neuroplasticity



How understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people:

People who think that the brain is fixed assume that talent is an inherent trait and that they will never become good at the things that they currently struggle with. Understanding the principles of neuroplasticity helps you realise that you have the power to change your brain if you want to, therefore you can learn and accomplish a lot more than you think!

How I might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for my benefit:

Remembering that intensity and the hard parts are actually the points where I am learning the most. Just because I struggle with something now doesn’t mean I always will & I will actually learn faster/more effectively if I recognise that I WILL know the thing at some point.

What are some ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

  • Meditation: teaches you to notice when your brain is going down the same pathways and helps reset the brain.
  • Exercises such as goal setting, self-reflection, etc.
  • Growth mindset.
  • Surrounding yourself with people who inspire/motivate you.
  • Taking care of your physical health (exercise, sleep).
  • Learning & practicing new things, e.g. music, art, coding ;), reading a book on an unfamiliar topic, a different language.
  • Not to be That Guy (I’m not a guy anyway) but psychedelics - under careful conditions, like with a spiritual guide or clinician & obviously in places where that is legal.

What is a growth mindset?

I see a growth mindset as something closely linked to neuroplasticity. A growth mindset is where you consciously maintain an attitude that your skill, talent, and capacity can change/improve over time.

It is SUPER relevant because we are doing this tricky AS course where not everybody has experience with coding or is that into problem solving. Instead of feeling stink about being in that position, anyone with a growth mindset will do really well in this course.

Growth mindsets are awesome beyond the scope of this course as well. Knowing how much we can learn and achieve over time will take us all super far in whatever areas we choose. Yaass.
